REP Instrumentation¶

REP is an open-source library for controlling lab instruments. It’s an alternative to LabVIEW [1] if you’d rather script your experiments in Python than fiddle with little wires and blocks.

REP makes it easy to interface your lab instruments with your data analysis and visualization tools: NumPy, SciPy, and Matplotlib.
